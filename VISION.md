# THREX — Product Vision
## *The Internet's Layer for Serious Minds*

> **Version:** 2.0 | **Status:** Living Document

---

## Executive Summary

Threx is a next-generation AI-native platform that unifies five previously separate categories of software — knowledge management, professional identity, intelligent matching, verifiable reputation, and autonomous AI agents — into a single compounding system.

The v2.0 vision expands on the original architecture with seven new capability pillars: real-time AI co-thinking, voice-to-node capture, autonomous publishing agents, idea longevity prediction, token-gated economic reputation, an intellectual services marketplace, and a full IP ownership layer. Together, these transform Threx from a platform people use into infrastructure people depend on.

> **The Core Thesis:** Every professional leaves behind a trail of thinking — notes, emails, conversations, presentations. Today, 98% of that thinking disappears. Threx makes intellectual output permanent, findable, attributed, and economically valuable. Your thinking compounds. Your reputation compounds. Your network compounds. None of it decays.

---

## The Problem

The knowledge economy has a fundamental infrastructure problem. The tools people use to think, share, and collaborate were built for different eras and different intentions. None of them were designed to make intellectual output permanently valuable.

**The five broken layers:**

- **Identity is performed, not proven.** LinkedIn rewards connection count and title prestige. There is no platform that answers: *"What has this person actually contributed to human knowledge?"*
- **Knowledge is siloed and mortal.** Notion, Obsidian, and Roam lock your best thinking in private archives that no one can discover, cite, or build upon. Ideas die in private folders.
- **Matching is surface-level.** Every professional network matches on demographics and credentials. None match on the content of your thinking — the actual intellectual surface area where collaboration happens.
- **Reputation is gamed.** Follower counts, LinkedIn endorsements, and citation counts are all easily manipulated. There is no verifiable, anti-gaming reputation signal for intellectual depth.
- **AI is a tool, not a collaborator.** Current AI tools answer questions and generate content. No platform uses AI to actively advance your thinking — debating your claims, predicting which ideas matter, and working autonomously on your behalf.

## Vision

A world where your intellectual output compounds over time. Where the ideas you publish today connect to people and opportunities you haven't imagined yet. Where reputation is earned, not performed. Where the right collaborator finds you before you even know you need them. Where thinking out loud creates real economic value.

### 1. 🪪 Living Identity — Your Proof of Work

Your Threx profile is not a resume. It is a live, AI-synthesized record of everything you have contributed, built, argued for, and been proven right about. The only professional identity in the world that gets more valuable the more you use it.

**Features:**
- **Contribution Timeline** — a chronological graph of every idea, project, prediction, and collaboration, automatically synthesized by your agent into a narrative of intellectual growth
- **Domain Reputation Scores** — per-domain scores (e.g., "Machine Learning: 847", "Bioethics: 312") updated in real time based on citations, prediction accuracy, collaboration outcomes, and peer engagement
- **Thinking Style Profile** — AI infers whether you are a systems thinker, a contrarian, a connector, or a synthesizer. Enriches matching beyond what job titles can
- **Import Layer** — connect GitHub, Google Scholar, arXiv, Substack, Notion to bootstrap your identity from existing work. AI reads, classifies, and imports your prior output as nodes

**NEW: Cross-Platform Graph Identity**

Your Threx knowledge graph becomes your universal professional identity — a live, embeddable widget showing your reputation scores, top nodes, and verified credentials. It replaces the PDF resume.

- Embeddable reputation card — paste into any website, GitHub README, or email signature
- API-verifiable identity — any third party can verify your Threx reputation score via a single API call
- Automatic CV generation — your agent generates a live, always-current CV from your graph on demand
- Interview mode — prospective employers or collaborators can query your agent about your work with your permission

**NEW: Verified Institutional Credentials**

Threx partners with universities, research institutions, YC, and professional bodies to issue verified credentials directly. Not self-reported — issued via Threx's credential API.

- Partner types: universities, research labs, accelerators (YC Africa), professional bodies (IEEE, AAAS)
- Credential types: degree verification, fellowship completion, research publication, accelerator alumni
- On-chain anchoring: all credentials timestamped and hashed to Base (Ethereum L2) for permanent verifiability

### 2. 🧠 Living Knowledge Graph — Ideas as Infrastructure

Every piece of content on Threx is a live node in a global knowledge graph. Ideas are never static. They evolve, connect, contradict, and compound. The graph is the product.

**Node Types:**
- **Claim** — a falsifiable statement with a resolution mechanism. Can be staked with reputation
- **Hypothesis** — a testable idea with attached evidence tracking
- **Question** — an open problem. Others submit answers, ranked by the community
- **Essay** — long-form reasoning with inline citations and version history
- **Data** — a dataset, finding, or empirical observation. Linked to external repositories
- **Project** — a live, collaborative workspace with goals, milestones, and outcome tracking
- **Summary** — a synthesis of external content attributed to the synthesizer

**AI-Powered Connection Engine** — automatically surfaces semantic relationships between your nodes and others' across the entire network, with plain-language reasons for every connection.

**Contradiction Flagging** — when new evidence contradicts a published claim, the node is flagged, the author is notified, and the contradiction is permanently attached to both nodes.

**Version History** — every node has a full edit history. See how an idea evolved and who influenced its evolution.

**NEW: Voice-to-Node Capture**

The single biggest barrier to knowledge capture is friction. Voice-to-Node eliminates it.

- Speak for 30 seconds — your agent transcribes, classifies, structures, and drafts a node
- Works in 40+ languages with special optimization for Yoruba, Igbo, Hausa, Swahili, and Amharic
- Ambient capture mode — leave it recording during a walk or meeting; the agent extracts ideas automatically
- Available on iOS, Android, and Chrome extension

**NEW: IP Ownership Layer**

Every published node receives an automatic cryptographic timestamp anchored to Base (Ethereum L2). A native prior art system for the internet.

- Every node gets a SHA-256 content hash and a blockchain timestamp on publish via EAS
- Timestamped PDF certificates downloadable for legal or institutional use
- If two people publish similar ideas, chain of timestamps establishes clear priority
- Citation tracking becomes attribution tracking — who built on what you owned first

### 3. 🤖 Real-Time AI Co-Thinking — The Engine Room

Not AI as an assistant. Not AI as a search engine. AI as an intellectual sparring partner that makes your thinking better in real time. This is the feature that defines Threx.

**Debate Mode**

As you write a node, Threx's AI takes the opposing position — surfacing the strongest counterarguments from the knowledge graph, academic literature, and its own reasoning. You finish writing a stronger claim because you've already survived the best challenge to it.

- **Socratic mode** — AI asks clarifying questions that force you to sharpen your reasoning
- **Steel-man mode** — AI constructs the strongest possible version of your argument before challenging it
- **Evidence mode** — AI surfaces supporting and contradicting evidence in real time as you write
- All AI challenges are logged as a structured debate trail attached to the node

**NEW: Idea Longevity Prediction**

Threx's AI analyzes your node across multiple dimensions — novelty, evidence quality, citation trajectory, domain velocity — and produces an **Idea Longevity Score**.

| Score Band | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Ephemeral | Trending but shallow. Value decays quickly. |
| Durable | Solid reasoning. Likely to compound over time. |
| Foundational | Likely to become a cited reference in its domain. |
| Contrarian | Low current consensus but historically high signal. |

- Updated dynamically as new evidence, citations, and contradictions emerge
- Used in feed ranking — Foundational ideas surface more to new users over time
- Used in matching — connects you with people working on high-longevity ideas in your domain

**NEW: Autonomous Agent Publishing**

For users who opt in, the agent can publish nodes within strict, user-defined constraints. Not automated spam — deliberate, supervised intellectual output.

- **Weekly synthesis node** — agent reviews private notes and drafts a synthesis for your review. One click to publish or discard
- **Citation response** — when your node is cited, agent drafts a response node acknowledging the citation and extending the argument
- **Research brief nodes** — when an agent research task completes, it drafts findings as a publishable node ready for your review
- All autonomous drafts clearly labeled as "Agent Draft" until approved by the human author
- Human approval is enforced at the infrastructure level — not just the UI

### 4. 🤝 Intelligent Matching Engine — The Right People, Always

The most sophisticated professional matching system ever built. Matches on the content of your thinking — the actual intellectual surface area where collaboration creates value.

**Matching Dimensions:**

| Dimension | Weight | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Semantic similarity | 35% | Cosine similarity between node embedding sets |
| Productive tension | 25% | Intentional mismatch on conclusions, alignment on methodology |
| Domain overlap | 20% | Weighted intersection of expertise, bonus for rare cross-domain combinations |
| Reputation proximity | 10% | Matches people at similar intellectual development stages |
| Activity recency | 10% | Deprioritizes inactive users; rewards active publishers |

**Match Explainability** — every match includes a plain-language reason written by Claude: why this person, why now, what the potential is.

**Tension Matching** — intentionally connects people with opposing but complementary views. *"You and Zara agree on the problem but diverge on the solution. That tension is productive."*

**NEW: Live Debate Rooms with Recorded Outcomes**

Structured intellectual discourse with real stakes and permanent records.

- Format: 1v1 or team debates on a specific claim or question. 60-minute sessions
- Structure: Opening positions (5 min each) → evidence rounds (3 × 10 min) → closing (3 min each) → community verdict
- Community verdict: 48-hour voting window. Voters must have domain reputation ≥ 100 to vote
- Outcome recording: verdicts are permanently attached to both participants' profiles. *"Won 14 debates on AI safety"* is a real credential
- Reputation stakes: both participants stake TRP before debating. Winner gains from loser's stake
- Full recording and transcript permanently attached to the debate node

### 5. 🎯 Intellectual Reputation Market — Signal Over Noise

Replaces follower counts and endorsements with a system that rewards quality of thinking, prediction accuracy, and depth of contribution. Anti-gameable by design.

**Reputation Events and Weights:**

| Event | Delta |
|---|---|
| Node published | +5 |
| Node cited by another user | +15 |
| Constructive reaction received | +3 |
| Claim resolved correctly | +25 |
| Prediction accurate | +30 |
| Collaboration completed | +20 |
| Peer review completed | +10 |
| Debate won | +stake amount |
| Claim resolved wrong | −10 |
| Prediction inaccurate | −15 |
| Debate lost | −stake amount |
| Node decay (inactive >90 days) | −1/week |
| Manipulation detected | −50 |

**NEW: Token-Gated Reputation Staking (TRP)**

An economic layer on top of reputation. TRP is an internal, non-speculative unit — it cannot be purchased with money, only earned through intellectual contribution.

- Stake TRP on claims, predictions, and debates
- Correct stakes earn from the pool of those who staked against
- Incorrect stakes lose to the pool
- TRP cannot be transferred, sold, or purchased — ever. Enforced at database constraint level, not just application level
- High-TRP users unlock gated features: Talent Access results, priority matching, ability to post Challenges with TRP prizes

**Reputation Badges:**

| Badge | Earned By |
|---|---|
| Early Caller | Staked on a claim before consensus formed and won |
| Constructive Contrarian | Challenged a high-reputation node with evidence that held up |
| Bridge Builder | Created a connection between two previously unlinked domains |
| Evidence Changer | Changed your publicly stated position based on new evidence — a badge of honor |
| Replication Champion | Successfully replicated 5+ external research findings |
| Debate Veteran | Participated in 20+ recorded debates |

**Anti-Gaming Layer:**
- AI detects coordinated reputation manipulation and sycophantic endorsement farms
- Neo4j cluster analysis runs nightly to detect suspicious mutual-boosting patterns
- Velocity limits: max 500 TRP staked in any 24-hour window
- New account boosting detection: high-value actions from accounts < 7 days old are quarantined

### 7. 🤖 Threx Agent v2.0 — Your Autonomous Intellectual Co-Pilot

The v2.0 agent has a persistent model of your intellectual goals. It monitors the world on your behalf, drafts on your behalf, and negotiates on your behalf.

**Agent Capabilities:**
- Real-time graph monitoring — surfaces connections to your graph within minutes of new publications
- Voice-to-node pipeline — transcribes, structures, and queues drafts from voice input
- Autonomous draft publishing — drafts nodes, responses, and syntheses for your review
- Grant and opportunity applications — identifies relevant opportunities, drafts materials using your graph as evidence, submits with your approval
- Meeting prep briefs — before any Threx connection meeting, generates a structured brief on the other person
- Weekly intelligence digest — domain developments, prediction resolutions, new matches, emerging opportunities
- Idea incubation — describe a half-formed idea; agent builds full context brief and surfaces the 3 people most likely to help you develop it

**NEW: Agent-to-Agent Protocol**

When two users' agents determine a high-value introduction is warranted, they negotiate the introduction before any human interaction occurs.

- Agent A identifies a potential match. Agent B reviews on behalf of User B and accepts or declines based on stated preferences
- If both agents agree, they draft a contextual introduction referencing the specific intellectual connection
- Humans review and approve before any message is sent
- Users configure their agent's preferences: domains it can accept introductions in, frequency cap, minimum reputation of proposed matches

## Competitive Moat

| Competitor | Gap | Threx's Advantage |
|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn | Static profiles, follower-count reputation | Knowledge graph, domain scores, AI matching on thinking content |
| Twitter / X | Viral mechanics, no depth | Reputation system, contradiction tracking, no engagement farming |
| Notion / Obsidian | Fully private, no social layer | Social graph, AI co-thinking, economic layer, ideas made public and valuable |
| ResearchGate | Narrow (academia only), outdated UX | Cross-domain, agent, marketplace, live papers, matching engine |
| Substack | One-way broadcast, no graph | Bidirectional knowledge graph, matching, reputation, co-authoring |
| GitHub | Code only, no cross-domain ideas | Cross-domain nodes, IP layer, non-developer communities |
| Perplexity / ChatGPT | No identity, no persistence, no reputation | Everything persists, everything attributes, everything compounds |

**The compounding moat:**
1. **Data flywheel** — more nodes → smarter matching → denser graph → more value per user
2. **Reputation lock-in** — a score built over years cannot be migrated to a competitor
3. **Graph stickiness** — a user's knowledge graph connected to hundreds of others' cannot be replicated
4. **Network effect** — value scales with graph density, not just user count. Superlinear returns
5. **IP layer permanence** — once users have timestamped IP on Threx, churn risk drops sharply

## Go-To-Market

### Phase 0 — Seed Community (Months 1–2)
Hand-pick 200 founding members from African tech, global AI research, and open science communities. Invite-only. Weekly calls. Product shaped by the cohort. Goal: 100 DAU with a dense knowledge graph before any press.

### Phase 1 — Private Beta (Months 3–4)
Founding members invite 3 people each. Core features live: knowledge graph, AI co-thinking, voice-to-node, basic matching, reputation v1. Press embargo. Goal: 1,000 users, 10,000 nodes, first $5K MRR.

### Phase 2 — Public Launch (Months 5–6)
ProductHunt launch. Partnership with 3 research institutions and one African university. Press: TechCrunch, Hacker News, African tech press. Paid tiers live. Debate rooms launched. Goal: 10,000 users, $15,000 MRR.

### Phase 3 — Scale (Months 7–18)
Native mobile apps. Org tier. Talent Access tier. Marketplace launch. IP layer with on-chain certificates. Series A. Goal: 100,000 users, $250,000 MRR.

**Growth Loops:**
- Shareable node cards with embedded reputation score — every published node is a Threx advertisement
- Embeddable reputation widget for GitHub READMEs, personal websites, and email signatures
- Weekly "Top Nodes in [Domain]" email to non-members who have engaged with public Threx content
- Live debate recordings — a great intellectual debate is more compelling than most podcast episodes
- Institutional loop — every university partnership makes an entire faculty and student body potential users

## Risks and Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Cold start — empty graph has no value | Hand-curated founding cohort ensures graph density before public launch |
| AI cost at scale | Aggressive caching, batch processing, tiered AI access by plan. Claude Haiku for low-stakes tasks, Sonnet only for user-facing output |
| TRP system gaming | Cannot be purchased or transferred. Velocity limits at DB level. Neo4j cluster detection nightly. Human audit weekly |
| Voice capture privacy | On-device processing where possible. Server-side encrypted. Full opt-out available |
| Regulation on IP timestamps | Timestamping supplements legal IP protection; it does not replace it. EAS attestations admissible as prior art evidence in most jurisdictions |
| Platform dependency on Anthropic/OpenAI | AI provider abstracted behind unified interface. Can switch to open-source models with minimal disruption |
| Talent competition for AI engineers | Moat is the graph data and reputation system — not the AI models. Anyone can call Claude; nobody else has this graph |

## Trust, Privacy, and Ethics

- **You own your graph** — all nodes, connections, and reputation data are exportable at any time in open formats
- **Opt-in visibility** — every node has granular visibility controls. Nothing is public by default
- **No dark patterns** — no infinite scroll, no algorithmic outrage, no engagement farming
- **Transparent AI** — every AI action is explainable and auditable. Every match, connection, and synthesis has a stored reason
- **Reputation integrity** — manipulation detection is a first-class engineering priority, not an afterthought
- **Right to forget** — users can delete their entire presence. The graph removes their nodes and recomputes affected connections
- **Autonomous publishing safety** — human approval is enforced at infrastructure level for all agent-drafted publish actions
